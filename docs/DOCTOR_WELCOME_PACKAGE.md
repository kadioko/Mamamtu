## System Overview

### What MamaMtu Does

1. **Patient Registration** - Digital forms replace paper cards
2. **ANC Visit Tracking** - Schedule and track all antenatal visits
3. **Risk Assessment** - Flag high-risk pregnancies automatically
4. **SMS Reminders** - Automatic appointment reminders to patients
5. **Reporting** - Generate reports for Ministry of Health

### How It Works

- **Web-based** - Works on any device with browser (phone, tablet, computer)
- **Offline capable** - No internet? No problem. Syncs when connection returns.
- **Swahili + English** - Interface switches language
- **Secure** - Encrypted, role-based access, audit logs

## FAQ for Clinical Staff

**Q: What if internet goes down?**  
A: System works offline. Data syncs automatically when connection returns.

**Q: Is patient data safe?**  
A: Yes. Encrypted, stored in EU (GDPR-compliant), only clinic staff can access.

**Q: Do patients need smartphones?**  
A: No. SMS reminders work on any phone. Patients don't need to use the app.

**Q: What about Ministry of Health reporting?**  
A: We generate the reports you need. Just print and submit.

**Q: Who owns the data?**  
A: The patient owns their data. The clinic is the data controller. We just process it.

**Q: What happens if we stop using MamaMtu?**  
A: We export all your data (Excel/PDF) for free. You keep everything.
