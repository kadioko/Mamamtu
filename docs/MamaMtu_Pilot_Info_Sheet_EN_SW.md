### MamaMtu — Free 3-Month Pilot for Your Clinic

**What is MamaMtu?**

MamaMtu is a secure digital system designed for clinics and hospitals in Tanzania to manage maternity patient records, track antenatal care visits, send appointment reminders, and generate Ministry of Health reports.

---

**Why Join the Pilot?**

| You Get | Details |
|---|---|
| Free access for 3 months | No cost. No commitment. |
| Staff training included | 2-hour session — we come to you |
| WhatsApp support | Priority response within 2 hours |
| Patient consent forms | Printed, bilingual (English + Swahili) |
| Privacy notice poster | For your waiting area |
| Tablet provided if needed | 1–2 devices if required |
| Full data export if you leave | All your data returned, free |

---

**What MamaMtu Does**

1. **Patient Registration** — Digital forms replace paper cards
2. **ANC Visit Tracking** — Schedule and track all antenatal visits
3. **High-Risk Flagging** — Automatically highlights high-risk pregnancies
4. **Appointment Reminders** — SMS reminders sent to patients automatically
5. **Reports** — Generate reports for Ministry of Health compliance

---

**How It Works**

- Works on any device: phone, tablet, or computer
- **Offline capable** — no internet? No problem. Syncs when connection returns.
- **Swahili + English** interface
- Encrypted and role-based — only your staff can see your patients

---

**What We Ask From You**

- Designate 1–2 staff to use it daily
- Enter at least 10 patient records per week
- Send brief weekly feedback (WhatsApp voice note is fine)
- Use with real patients who have signed consent forms

---

**After 3 Months**

- Continue free (up to 100 patients)
- Upgrade to Pro: TZS 100,000–150,000/month
- Or exit — we return all your data at no cost

No automatic billing. No surprise charges.

### MamaMtu — Majaribio ya Bure ya Miezi 3 kwa Kliniki Yako

**MamaMtu ni nini?**

MamaMtu ni mfumo salama wa kidijitali ulioundwa kwa ajili ya kliniki na hospitali Tanzania kusimamia rekodi za wajawazito, kufuatilia ziara za kliniki ya ujauzito (ANC), kutuma ukumbusho wa miadi, na kutengeneza ripoti za Wizara ya Afya.

---

**Kwa nini jiunge na Majaribio?**

| Unapata | Maelezo |

| Ufikiaji wa bure kwa miezi 3 | Bila gharama. Bila wajibu. |
| Mafunzo ya wafanyakazi | Saa 2 — tunakuja kwako |
| Msaada wa WhatsApp | Majibu ya haraka ndani ya saa 2 |

**MamaMtu Inafanya Nini**

1. **Usajili wa Mgonjwa** — Fomu za kidijitali zinachukua nafasi ya kadi za karatasi
2. **Ufuatiliaji wa Ziara za ANC** — Panga na ufuatilie ziara zote za ujauzito
3. **Alama ya Hatari** — Inaonyesha mimba zenye hatari moja kwa moja
4. **Ukumbusho wa Miadi** — Ujumbe mfupi (SMS) unatumwa kwa wagonjwa moja kwa moja
