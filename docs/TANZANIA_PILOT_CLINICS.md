# Tanzania Pilot Clinic Outreach List

**Purpose:** Target clinics for MamaMtu beta pilot program (free for 3 months, then transition to paid).

**Last Updated:** 2026-05-28

---

## Target Criteria

**Ideal Pilot Clinic:**
- 5-50 staff members (manageable size for feedback)
- Serves maternal health patients (ANC, delivery, postnatal)
- Some digital readiness (at least one computer/tablet + internet)
- Located in Dar es Salaam initially (easier for in-person support)
- Decision-maker accessible (owner, administrator, or medical director)
- Open to trying new systems (early adopter mindset)

## Tips for Tanzania Clinic Outreach

1. **Lead with relationships**
   - Warm introductions beat cold emails
   - Ask your network: "Do you know anyone who works at a clinic?"

2. **Visit in person**
   - Many clinic administrators don't check email regularly
   - A respectful in-person visit can be more effective

3. **Speak Swahili**
   - Even if the administrator speaks English, starting in Swahili builds rapport

4. **Emphasize free pilot**
   - "No cost, no commitment for 3 months" lowers barriers

5. **Show, don't just tell**
   - Bring a tablet, show the demo live
   - Let them touch and use it

6. **Address concerns proactively**
   - "What if internet goes down?" → Show offline capability
   - "What about patient privacy?" → Show compliance docs
   - "What if we don't like it?" → No obligation after 3 months

7. **Follow up persistently**
   - Healthcare administrators are busy
   - Polite follow-up after 3-5 days is normal
