What is MamaMtu?

A secure digital platform for clinics and hospitals in Tanzania to manage maternity patient records, track antenatal care (ANC) visits, send appointment reminders, and generate Ministry of Health reports.

What You Get — Free for 3 Months

  • ✓
    Full platform access — all features, no restrictions
  • ✓
    Staff training — 2-hour session, we come to you
  • ✓
    WhatsApp support — priority response within 2 hours
  • ✓
    Patient consent forms — printed, English + Swahili
  • ✓
    Privacy notice poster — for your waiting area
  • ✓
    Tablet provided — 1–2 devices if needed
  • ✓
    Full data export on exit — all your data returned, free

What MamaMtu Does

  1. Patient Registration — digital forms replace paper cards
  2. ANC Visit Tracking — schedule and record all antenatal visits
  3. High-Risk Flagging — automatically highlights at-risk pregnancies
  4. Appointment Reminders — SMS sent to patients automatically
  5. MoH Reports — generate Ministry of Health compliance reports

How It Works

Any device Works offline Swahili + English Encrypted Role-based access

What We Ask From You

Your commitment during the pilot
  • Designate 1–2 staff members to use it daily
  • Enter at least 10 patient records per week
  • Send brief weekly feedback (voice note is fine)
  • Use with real patients who have signed consent forms
